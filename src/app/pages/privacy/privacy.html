App Permissions

What Permissions We Request & Why

Contacts (Optional)
Used only when you choose to import a single contact for a loan entry. Your full contact list is never read, uploaded, or stored. You can deny this permission and enter details manually.
Notifications (Optional)
Used to send you local reminders about loan due dates and scheduled expense alerts. Push notifications can be disabled at any time from device settings.
Internet Access (Required)
Required for account login/registration, Google Sign-In, fetching live currency exchange rates, and sending loan reminders. Core financial tracking works fully offline.
Local Storage (Required)
Used to store your financial data — expenses, income, notes, and settings — entirely on your device. This data is never uploaded to any server.
